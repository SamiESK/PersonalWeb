Project Details

The owner of Clips media company desired an application where it can utilize trending or viral videos as its product for the company. The desires services was an algorithm that communicates with TikTok's API and collects 'important' videos done without storing anything in a database. This was done in Python while also using mathematical ratios done by the marketing team that classifies 'important' products as potential future product to advertise. Collected video are later displayed for the company to through all potential products deriving by the newly created algorithm.
